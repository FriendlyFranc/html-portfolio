The Best Movies According to Wynand

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spirit

This movie touched me as a child, because of its beautiful plot majestic scenes, where the protagonist is finally free.

Pacific Rim

This movie has amazing CGI, with epic battles between humungous robots and alien creatures, that escape from the Barrier Reef.

The Irishman

Robert Deniro plays the role so well in this movie. The setting and theme is so enjoyable. Highly recommend.
